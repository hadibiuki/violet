The Violet brand lockup — a gradient "V" tile plus the tracked VIOLET wordmark; use it in nav bars, footers, login screens, and any brand moment.

```jsx
<BrandMark />
<BrandMark size="lg" tone="onDark" />
<BrandMark showWord={false} />   {/* tile only, e.g. compact app header */}
```

- `tone="onDark"` switches the wordmark to white for the celestial hero/footer.
- The tile is always the violet gradient; never recolor it. There is no raster logo — this lockup *is* the mark.
