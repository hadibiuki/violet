Colors

foundations

Violet-tinted neutrals carry 60% of every screen, ink type & structure 30%, brand violet ~10%, gold a fraction of that. Pick by role, never by hex.

Typography

foundations

Cormorant Garamond for brand moments; Inter (EN/RU), Vazirmatn (FA), Cairo (AR) for UI; JetBrains Mono for SKUs.

Spacing & elevation

foundations

A 4px base scale, brand radii, violet-tinted shadows, and purposeful motion.
